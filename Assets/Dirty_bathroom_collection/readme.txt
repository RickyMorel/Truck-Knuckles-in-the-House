This package contains 21 different types of dirty bathroom objects like toilette, sink, shower and bathtube.
Just drag the prefabs into your scene and arrange them as you like.

You may also want to have a look at the example scene that contains all the prefabs.
Textures with different colors and levels of dirt are included.

All single parts using about 1300-2500 Polygons.

Textures are baked with ambient occlusion.

The original FBX-file is also included in the package.
